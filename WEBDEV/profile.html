Johnny Makasalanan

Racer ng Bayan, Taga-linis ng sasakyan

"Sabi nila, ang bilis daw ng oras. Eh paano kung race car driver ka? Bilis ng oras, pero mas bilis ako sa racetrack!" -Johnny Makasalanan

"As a professional racer, I specialize in precision driving, strategic pit stops, and maintaining composure at breakneck speeds—combining the finesse of a ballet dancer with the horsepower of a thoroughbred. In contrast, as a car wash boy, I bring a meticulous touch to every vehicle, ensuring each one leaves with a shine brighter than a supernova, proving that attention to detail extends beyond the racetrack"

Work Experience

As a professional racer, I mastered the art of navigating racetracks with the precision of a surgeon, turning each lap into a high-speed ballet. Meanwhile, my stint as a car wash aficionado honed my skills in coaxing every vehicle to gleam like a freshly polished gem, proving that my attention to detail isn't limited to the fast lane. From burning rubber to buffing bumpers, I've embraced every aspect of the automotive world with flair and finesse.
